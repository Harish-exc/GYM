Walking, Mobility & Stretching

  1. Walking — 30 to 45 minutes
  2. Mobility: neck rotations, shoulder circles, arm swings, hip circles, ankle rotations — 2 sets each
  3. Stretching: chest, lats, hamstrings, quads, calves — 30 sec × 3 each
  4. Optional: light cycling, light jogging, or an easy sport
Goal: Let muscles, joints, and the nervous system recover while staying active. This is not a training day — keep effort low.
